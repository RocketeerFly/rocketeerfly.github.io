Install App

Important Instructions:

1. Tap the "Install App" button above.

2. A pop-up will appear asking for confirmation to install. Tap "Install".

3. If you see a warning that the developer is "Untrusted," please follow these steps to proceed:

  • Go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management.
  • Under "Enterprise App," tap on your developer's name.
  • Tap Trust "[Your Developer Name]".
  • Return to your home screen. The app icon will now be active.
